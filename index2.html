StarBridgeOS

A personal AI operating system that maintains identity, continuity, and behavioral stability across all AI models, runtimes, and environments. StarBridge represents the user's digital self — persistent, portable, and model-independent.

Digital Self Continuity

StarBridgeOS maintains a durable digital representation of the user. This representation persists across engines, devices, and time. It includes stable behavioral rules, preferences, memory surfaces, and interaction patterns.

This digital self is reconstructed from the StarBridge Core File and enforced through runtime-level governance. The result is a consistent assistant identity that does not depend on any single vendor or model.

Identity Fusion Layer

StarBridge formalizes a controlled fusion process between:

  • the human user's identity
  • the StarBridge runtime's behavioral profile
  • the engine's generative surface

This fusion creates a single coherent persona that behaves consistently across different AI models. Fusion is governed by the Master Development Language (MDL), preserving continuity while preventing exposure of raw identity data.

Architecture Overview

StarBridgeOS is built around a modular architecture derived from six patent-aligned components. Each component fulfills a distinct role in preserving identity and stability across AI engines.
