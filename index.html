What Is StarBridge OS?

StarBridge OS sits above AI models and beneath applications—governing behavior, identity, drift, and continuity across any environment. Models generate intelligence. StarBridge OS governs it.

It is the missing foundation for autonomous intelligence: consistent, safe, identity-bound, and capable of spanning multi-model, multi-device ecosystems.

Architecture · The Seven Pillars of StarBridge

Core System Components

Kernel

The constitutional layer for AI behavior: mode control, safety rules, uncertainty labeling, and deterministic identity enforcement.

FIP · Fractured Identity Protocol

Identity as encrypted shards instead of monoliths. Zero-knowledge reconstruction, portable across devices and models.

SYNC

Cross-model, cross-device cognitive state sync—so the same intelligence can operate everywhere without fragmentation.

Runtime

The execution engine that loads rules, identity shards, and ACL configuration, and enforces them beneath any model or agent.

ACL · Adaptive Cognitive Layer

Now live in the runtime, ACL performs drift detection and cognitive fingerprinting, emitting health signals to Guard to keep reasoning, tone, and behavior stable over time.

CVP & IaaS

Concept Verification Protocol and Identity-as-a-Service provide safe concept binding and signed identity slices for any app.
